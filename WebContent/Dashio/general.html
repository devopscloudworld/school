Maths Club

If you are planning on making a career in engineering and other professional fields, excellent mathematical skills are necessary. You can acquire high-grade skills by joining a good math club. There are several math clubs you can join online like All India Ramanujan Maths Club(AIRMS) and Indian Mathematical Society. Also, your school or college may have one.

Benifit 1

Encourage a positive attitude towards mathematics.

Benifit 2

Stimulate mathematical curiosity

Benifit 3

Inspire thinking,challenge and collaboration.

Benifit 4

Promote cross-curricular thinking

Benifit 5

Develop a healthy interest in mathematics
