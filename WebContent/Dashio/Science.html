Science And Astrology Clubs

Science Club

While your school or college will have a science club, you can also join an outside one. Lots of online scientific clubs that suit your likes are available nowadays. You can conduct experiments and participate in group projects..

There are multiple science clubs in India like Vigyan Prasar, Science Society which students should try indulging in their tender stage of life.

Astrology Club

Stargazing can be very interesting. Join an astronomy club to learn the movement of planets and stars, know more about galaxies and see hidden wonders of space.

There are countless astronomy clubs in India. You might get lucky and find an inhabited planet.
